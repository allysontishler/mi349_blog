Ally's Skincare Background

I grew up in a Korean American household and with the rising popularity of Korean skin care in recent years, I thought I would share my experiences through this blog. Throughout my childhood, I had severe hormonal and genetic acne that caused my skin to break out in painful patterns. I tried many of the top recommended products and personalized formulas, but nothing seemed to make a lasting impact. Around my pre-teens, I delved into the world of Korean skincare and never looked back. I spent my days researching what to look for in products and what not to look for. All of this led to the strengthening, brightening, and improved health of my skin. I still have break outs here and there, but I’m still on my journey to finding the perfect routine for me. With this blog, I hope that you can achieve this too!
